    Data Management

    Manage sites and site data.

    You can:

    • Add new sites to the databse
    • Upload images to existing sites
    • View and edit site details

    Site details available:

    • Site name
    • Location
    • Latitude and longitude coordinates
    • Elevation
    • Dominant deciduous broadleaf species
    • Date range of site images

    When images are uploaded they are classified as either occurring during the
    bud burst phenophase or the leaf senescence phenophase, using a machine
    learning classification model.

    The transition dates of the phenophases are then estimated from the
    series of classified images, the analysis of which can be viewed under "Analysis."
